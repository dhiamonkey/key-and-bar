Definition of Legal Counsel

The term lawyer retainer or lawyer retainer may not be very familiar to the public. What most people know is that a lawyer plays a role in providing legal assistance to his clients. However, not many know how a lawyer's system works.


Returning to the term retainer attached to the profession of lawyers, this term actually refers to a system in providing legal aid services, the work system of retainer lawyers is to provide legal assistance to their clients, with a certain period of time. Generally, this period lasts from 6 months to 5 years. The retainer system is quite beneficial for clients. The reason is that the client does not have to spend too much money to use legal aid services on a regular basis, both in judicial and non-judicial channels, compared to the costs that must be incurred, if the client incurs costs for each legal consultation or case handling.


This retainer lawyer service is widely used by companies, but not a few individuals also request legal assistance with this system. Generally, retainer lawyer services will be sought when there are problems encountered or there are legal issues that companies or individuals want to consult.
